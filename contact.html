Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent issues, please mark your email subject with "URGENT".

How do I request a refund?

Email scholara@gmail.com within 7 days of your purchase with your account email and reason for the refund. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers.

How long do study guides take?

Custom study guides are typically completed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification when your study guide is ready.

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your Account Settings. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What file formats do you accept for notes?

We accept PDF, DOCX, TXT, JPG, and PNG files. Maximum file size is 10MB per file.
